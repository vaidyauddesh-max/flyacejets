What Is the Fly ACEJETS Jet Card?

The Jet Card gives clients a structured way to plan private travel through selected flying-hour packages and preferred aircraft categories. It is suitable for clients who fly regularly and want a smoother enquiry, planning, and charter coordination experience.

Every journey is coordinated through the Fly ACEJETS charter desk with attention to routing, passenger needs, aircraft category, availability, operator approvals, and service expectations.
